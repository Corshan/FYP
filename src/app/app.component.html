Project Abstract

Chaos Chefs is a Virtual Reality (VR) cooking simulator game, the player takes on the role of a chef in a fast pace kitchen. The game has a multiplayer feature where players can join each other and cook recipes together. Each round of the game will last between 5 and 10 minutes, during which the players will have multiple customers ordering burgers with different toppings. The players will need to grill the burger patties, chop tomatoes, onions, pickles and then put all the ingredients together on a plate but customers are inpatient and if the players takes too long to complete their order. The customer will get angry and leave. When the players get the burgers out on time, with the correct toppings, the players will earn cash to upgrade their kitchens.
